Our Story

About Arikho
Suites

Who We Are

Bulawayo's Premier
Executive Suites

Arikho Suites was born from a vision to offer something different in Bulawayo — accommodation that genuinely feels like home, yet delivers the standards of a world-class property.

Located in the vibrant heart of Bulawayo, our six executive apartments are designed for the modern professional who values comfort, privacy, and self-sufficiency. Each suite is fully equipped — kitchen, lounge, dining area, bedrooms with en-suite — so you can work, eat, relax, and rest exactly as you choose.

Our Promise

What Sets Us Apart

01
Genuine Warmth

A warm smile and friendly demeanour greets every guest at our front desk. We believe hospitality begins with people.

02
Home Comforts

Self-catering apartments with fully equipped kitchens mean you eat on your schedule, cook your favourites, and truly settle in.

03
Your Safety

24-hour security and secure parking ensure you and your belongings are protected around the clock, every night of your stay.

04
Every Detail

From crisp white linen to DSTV in every lounge, laundry services to a professional chef on call — we've thought of everything.

Whether you need a professional chef to prepare your meals, laundry handled discreetly, or airport transfers arranged — our team goes the extra mile.
